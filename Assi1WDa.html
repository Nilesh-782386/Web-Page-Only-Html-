ABOUT US

Microsoft Learn Student Club is a technical club dedicated to elevating the coding culture at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology. We mentor and motivate members to refine their critical thinking, logical reasoning, and coding skills. Our goal is to spread technical knowledge through peer-to-peer learning, open source projects, technical events, and comprehensive seminars.

MLSC organizes impactful events like BIT by BIT and Capture the Flag, featuring expert-led sessions. These events help participants enhance vocational skills and problem-solving through curated content.
